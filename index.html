What Exactly...
Is a Tarea?

Technically speaking, it's a Spanish word that's pronounced tuh-ray-uh, but its meaning is simply "task".
Tarea is the name of our service, but it's really anything you need to get done.
Got homework? You've got yourself a Tarea. Big Meeting? Tarea.
We wanted to use a very nonspecific or general word for our idea, as Tarea (the app) can be used in a lot of ways.
